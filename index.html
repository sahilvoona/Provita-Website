Our Products

Here are some of the products we are working on at Provita.

MeshCare

MeshCare is a gel-based, biosynthetic and biocompatible mesh, mainly utilized for hernia meshes. Using silver-nanoparticles, it encourages antibody production and healing. It introduces a new method of treating hernias while reducing the risk of future complications also providing a product-life longer than any other on the market. MeshCare, compared to polypropylene and animal-based dermis competitors, significantly reduces the likelihood of future surgeries, lowering the financial stress on the final consumer.

FlaviVax

FlaviVax is a vaccine intended to treat the West Nile virus, specifically in third world countries in Africa. The focus of the product is to address problems within the ability to properly transport without cold-chain infrastructure, which requires supercooled fridges. This is to keep the vaccine preserved until administration. FlaviVax will use already established methods of preserving vaccines, namely the use of a mixture of Trehalose and Pullulan to create a pellet that can be reconstituted into a vaccine. The pellet can be stored for many weeks at temperatures near 40° celsius, and healthcare workers will be able to reconstruct it into a vaccine that will be taken orally in order to build immunity to the West Nile virus. Currently, the team is collaborating to finalize research initiatives regarding the formation of the pellet and its eventual reconstitution.

PatchAid

Patchaid is a patch utilizing transdermal technology to heal short and long-term hypertrophic scars. With a wide range of medical and cosmetic features, Patchaid aims to heal scars from acne, burning, and injuries with a simple, odorless, and undetectable delivery. With transdermal technology, Patchaid can seamlessly distribute the drug to the user through painless microneedles. Once the application is complete, the product changes colors to alert users when to take off the patch. Beyond its practicality, Patchaid is incredibly clean, biodegradable, and safe, featuring antifungals to prevent rashes and fungus that can occur. However, some side effects can occur including infections, allergies, and irritation. People with skin disorders are advised to not use this patch. What makes Patchaid different, reliable, and innovative? It’s the fact that Patchaid can heal long-term scars in addition to short-term — which current products on the market fail to accomplish. Utilizing a revolutionary combination of neprilysin encased in nanoparticles, Patchaid offers a simple solution to a persistent and wide-ranging problem.
